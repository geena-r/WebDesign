About

The Transportation Project, a non-profit born in the heart of New York City in 2024, is dedicated to advocating for the universal benefits of transportation. We firmly believe that access to safe, reliable, and efficient transportation is a fundamental right, essential for fostering equitable opportunities and sustainable communities. Our mission is clear: to create a future where transportation serves as a catalyst for progress and equality. We champion the expansion of transportation networks, advocating for improved subway lines and enhanced bus services, particularly in underserved areas like Queens. Through our advocacy, we strive to ensure that all individuals have access to the opportunities and resources that transportation provides. As we navigate the complex landscape of transportation issues, we remain committed to integrity and transparency. We provide our supporters with factual information, empowering them to make informed decisions and take meaningful action. Together, we work towards a more accessible, equitable, and sustainable future for all. Join us on this journey as we pave the way for a transportation system that truly serves the needs of every individual and community. Together, we can make a difference and create a brighter future for generations to come.
